Welcome to n1mmelUI!

n1mmelUI is a lightweight, Vanilla+ style UI enhancement designed to keep the original Blizzard feel while sanding off the rough edges. It adds a collection of highly requested Quality-of-Life features, equipment checks, and chat improvements without cluttering your screen.

Every feature is completely modular and can be easily toggled via the clean, standalone in-game settings menu.

Type /n1  in chat to open the options!

 

✨ Key Features
 

Quality of Life & Automations

Auto-Repair & Auto-Sell: Automatically repair your gear and sell gray junk items when visiting a merchant (with clean chat summaries).
Cinematic Skipper: Automatically skip cinematics and movies.
Hide Talking Head: Disable the annoying pop-up dialogue frames in dungeons and world quests.
AFK Screensaver: A neat visual screensaver with a spinning camera when you go AFK.
Equipment & Item Management

Item Level Display: Shows the actual item level directly on your gear in the Character Panel, Inspect Frame, and inside your Bags & Bank.
Missing Enchant/Gem Warning: Instantly see if a piece of gear is missing an enchant (shows a red 'X').
Manual Item Check: Run a full scan of your current gear to verify if everything is perfectly socketed and enchanted. Outputs to chat or a dedicated window.
Chat Enhancements

Clickable URLs: Web links in chat are highlighted in cyan. Clicking them opens a small window to easily copy the link (Ctrl+C).
Short Channel Names: Cleans up your chat by changing e.g., [Guild] to [G] and [Party] to [P].
Class Colors in Chat: Applies class colors to player names across all chat channels.
Whisper Alerts: Plays a customizable sound when you receive a character or Battle.net whisper.
Map & Minimap Options

Square Minimap: Option to switch from the default round minimap to a larger, clean square design.
Coordinates: Displays customizable X/Y coordinates on the World Map (Player & Cursor) and a small coordinate tracker below the Minimap.
UI & Performance

Class Colored Health Bars: Colors the default Blizzard player and target health bars according to their class.
Global Font Changer: Change the default UI font (Standard, PT Sans Narrow, or Expressway).
Performance Monitor: A sleek, built-in display for your current FPS, Latency (Home/World), and Addon Memory Usage.
🌍 Fully Localized n1mmelUI automatically detects your client language and fully supports: English, German, French, Spanish, and Russian.
